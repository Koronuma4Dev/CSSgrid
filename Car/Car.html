Honda civic Car list by generation

First Generation (1972-1979)

Models: 2-door sedan, 3-door hatchback.

Specifications: 1.2L and 1.5L engines, front-wheel drive.

Notable Features: Compact design, economic fuel consumption.

Second Generation (1979-1983)

Models: 3-door hatchback, 4-door sedan.

Specifications: Increased engine sizes (up to 1.5L), CVCC technology for fuel efficiency.

Notable Features: Updated design, increased dimensions for better space.

Third Generation (1983-1987)

Models: 4-door sedan, 5-door wagon, CRX coupe.

Specifications: 1.3L and 1.5L engines, new double-wishbone suspension.

Notable Features: Introduction of the sporty Civic CRX.

Fourth Generation (1987-1991)

Models: Hatchback, sedan, CRX.

Notable Features: Advanced aerodynamics, sporty Civic Si model.

Fifth Generation (1991-1995)

Models: Hatchback, sedan, coupe.

Specifications: VTEC engines in higher trims, up to 1.6L.

Notable Features: Introduction of safety upgrades and improved handling.
